# Hola España - Project Overview

## Project Summary

**Client:** Hola España Legal and Relocation Experts  
**Project Type:** Complete website design, development, and brand strategy  
**Timeline:** 6-7 weeks  
**Budget:** $5,500 total project value  
**Relationship:** Friends/family - trading services for immigration legal work

---

## Business Context

### What Hola España Does

Hola España provides legal immigration and relocation services for US citizens moving to Spain. Services include:

- Visa and residency applications
- Real estate legal support
- Complete relocation packages
- Immigration legal counsel

### Target Audience

**Primary:** High net-worth individuals and families

- Age: 35-65
- Income: $150k+
- Looking for premium, white-glove service
- Need reliable legal guidance for complex moves

### Business Goals

1. **Positioning:** Move from general service to premium provider
2. **Lead Generation:** Attract higher-value clients
3. **Authority:** Establish as the go-to Spain immigration expert
4. **Education:** Provide valuable content to build trust

## Technical Approach

### Technology Choices

**Next.js 14+ (App Router)**

- Modern React framework
- Great SEO out of the box
- Fast, performant
- Easy to deploy

**Tailwind CSS v4**

- CSS-first configuration
- Rapid development
- Consistent design system
- Small production bundle

**Sanity CMS**

- Headless CMS
- Real-time content editing
- Great UX for non-technical users
- Flexible content modeling

**React Spring**

- Lightweight animations
- Physics-based, natural feel
- Better performance than alternatives
- Scroll-triggered effects

**Vercel**

- Zero-config deployment
- Automatic HTTPS
- Built-in analytics
- Edge network for speed

### Why These Choices?

1. **Client Needs:** Non-technical client needs easy content management
2. **Performance:** Fast loading for SEO and user experience
3. **Modern:** Using latest best practices and tech
4. **Maintainable:** Well-documented, standard approaches
5. **Scalable:** Can grow with business needs

## Working Relationship

### Communication

**Primary Contact:** Client (Attorney)  
**Communication Method:** Email, phone calls, occasional video  
**Cadence:** Weekly check-ins, as-needed for decisions

### Decision Making

**Quick Decisions:** Design tweaks, color adjustments, content order  
**Slower Decisions:** Major features, pricing strategy, package structure

### Feedback Loop

1. We create something
2. Share with client for review
3. Client provides feedback
4. We iterate
5. Client approves
6. Move forward

**Philosophy:** Friends/family project with professional standards. Be patient, be flexible, but maintain quality.

## Risk Management

### Potential Issues

**Scope Creep**

- Solution: Refer to contract, document everything, gentle reminders

**Content Delays**

- Solution: Use placeholder content, build structure first

**Technical Challenges**

- Solution: Playground for testing, incremental approach

**Client Availability**

- Solution: Async communication, don't block on approvals

**Feature Requests**

- Solution: "Phase 2" list, separate quotes for additions
